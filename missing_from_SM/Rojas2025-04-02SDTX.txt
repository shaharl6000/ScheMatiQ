ORDER
Rolando Olvera, United States District Judge
*1 On this day came to be heard Petitioner and Respondents on the petition for a writ of habeas corpus pursuant to 28 U.S.C. § 2241. The Court holds that Petitioner is a Venezuelan national with valid Temporary Protected Status and was wrongfully detained under 8 U.S.C. 1254a(a)(1)(A). The Court further holds that Respondents produced no evidence that Petitioner is a danger to the public. The Respondents arrested Petitioner in New York, and transferred him to Brownsville, Texas.
 
Thus, it is hereby ORDERED that Respondents, at their expense, return Petitioner to the city of his arrest in New York, as soon as reasonably possible. It is further ORDERED that Petitioner be released on his own recognizance as soon as possible, with the recommendation that an ankle monitor be placed on Petitioner pending his immigration hearing.
